Chromosome

The purpose of the first project was to analyze transcription factor binding sites clusters along a genome. Genome could be represented in linear coordinates from 0 to the length of a genome. Genes are positioned along the genome and characterized by three parameters. START, END and DIRECTION. Transcription factors are proteins that bind to specific linear sequences of nucleotides in genome. We were interested in finding areas of genome where transcription factor binding sites form clusters. Hypothetically, high local concentration of DNA bound transcription factors in areas like this will turn genes on and off with a higher probability than lone binding site.
