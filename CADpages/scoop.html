More Info:


This was probably the first CAD assignment in which I had to put some thought into the model, while taking the introductory course. My first attempt when making this model involved creatimng one face and then extruding the model half-way in an attempt to later mirror the part to get a whole piece. However, this caused issues with shelling the model. Instead I extruded the face the entire required length. Following this, I created rectangular subtraction extrusion to erase the top half and then shelled the middle of the crane. This as I was still learning basic CAD at the time, this model took me about an hour. With experience a model like this can be done much faster.
