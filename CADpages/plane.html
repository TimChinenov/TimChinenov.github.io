Warhammer Thunderbolt

Estimated Time:

3 weeks

Unique Parts:

20

Difficutly:

hard

More Info:


This model is based on the Imperial Thunderbolt from the Warhammer 40,000 universe. This is the most difficult piece I've done so far in Nx. Parts of the plane were not necessarily difficult, but the amount of detail was quite tedious. Every single bolt, rivet, and nail on the model was accounted for. The model required a more thorough knowledge of Nx. In the process of making this model I had to learn how to properlly project curves on to non flat surfaces for the wing engines and how to use the helix function to create the ribbed pipes.
