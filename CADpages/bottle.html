Square Bottle

Estimated Time:

1 hour

Unique Parts:

2

Difficutly:

Easy

More Info:


This was the first model I made after completing my CAD for engineers class. The bottle was based on a square potion bottle I have on my desk. The model is seriers of extrusions on top for extrusions. The base is a sqaure extrusion while the top portion is two different sized circle extrusions. The cork was made as seperate part. The cork is two circles connected by a through curve.
