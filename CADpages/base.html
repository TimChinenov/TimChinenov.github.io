More Info:


This model was a practice in more complicated extrusion shapes. Making the angled piece was a little awkward as there were several ways of approuching the given schematic. I first drew the right side of the spindle base and extruded this sketch. This gave a rectangular top which I had to edge blend to get the smooth circular surface. The triangle was sketched on a plane that was positioned in the center of the base and then extruded in both directions. The rest were simple surface extrusions.
