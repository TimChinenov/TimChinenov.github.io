More Info:


This basic mouse was a practice in not using sketches. Instead of using a Datum CSYS I tried to simply draw on created planes. The sketches were then mirrored, moved, or copied. The body of the mouse was created by a through curve connecting a series of skeleton sketches. The mouse body is also made entirely using sheet metal in Nx instead of extruding solids. Overall, this model was made as a learning experience in some of the other features in Nx and was not meant to be incredibly complicated.
