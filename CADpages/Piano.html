Piano Toy

Estimated Time:

12 hours

Unique Parts:

10

Difficutly:

Medium

More Info:


This model is not based of off grand piano, but a toy model of a grand piano that I keep on my desk. This is probably more clear if you look at the inside of the piano. I used a caliper to make all the measurements in cm. Some Measurements, especially the position of the holes had to be guessed as there was no clear pattern on the toy itself. Overall, I wouldn't consider this assembly to be considerably hard. However, the entire model was extremely time consuming.
