More Info:


This speaker was a practice in using a through curves. Two sketches were made a certain distance away from each other. The sketches were the front and back faces of the speaker. Then, using a through curve by points, the two sketches were connected. This is a very simple model, the only trick is probably fiddling with the points in the through curve.
