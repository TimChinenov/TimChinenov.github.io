Tri-Pod Cap

Estimated Time:

8 hour

Unique Parts:

1

Difficutly:

Medium-hard

More Info:


This Tripod cap is probably the first complicated model I had to do and involved a lot of trial and error. As expected, the schematics were not incredibly clear on some of the features. The most trouble came from the smooth curved top. After trying the get this smooth curve using different methods, I went with a cross section sketch of y,z plane of the tripod revolved around the z axis. Unfortunately this resulted in a cylinder, but it gave the curved top result. To get the cylinder into a the three pointed star shape I sketched the three pointed star on the bottom of the cylinder and extruded erased all the excess. The inside of this shape was shelled out. Overall the model might have taken a little over 8 hours due to all the trial error and the misuderstanding of parts of the schematics.
