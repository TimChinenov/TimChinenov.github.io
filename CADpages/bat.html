More Info:


This paddle was a test in long extrusion and how to compensate for long extrusion in sketches. The paddle was created with a series of sketches that were done on the previously extruded piece. First I sketched the base handle of the paddle and extruded that sketch. At the end of that extrusion I did the next sketch, extruded the sketch, and so on. Because od the awkward length of the paddle, in the sketch I had to use a split function which shortened the long constant section of the paddle.
