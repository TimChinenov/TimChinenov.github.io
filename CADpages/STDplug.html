More Info:


Following the USB and the AUX plug, the AC plug was the next model in my series plugs. This plug was also measured using a set of calipers, increasing the time to complete this model greatly. This plug also had some surved features which were difficult to measure, such as the smooth indent in the center of the plug. Used a spline in order to get the swept guide fo this section. Of the three plugs I made, this one was probably the least enjoyable one due to its awkward mix of curves and rectangular body.
