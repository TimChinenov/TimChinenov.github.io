More Info:


This USB along with the AUX plug and the AC plug was part of the cable series I worked on after I finished my second semester at RPI. Just like the other two chords I did all the measurements myself with a set of a calipers. Making all the measurements myself increased the length to complete this USB. The USB symbol on the top of the model also proved to be quite difficult. I had to redo the logo several times because I didn't like how it looked or my measurements were not exact. I made the inside of the USB as seperate piece. The inside is a very simple representation of the inside piece at the USB's tip.
